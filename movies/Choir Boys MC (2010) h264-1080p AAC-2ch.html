Choir Boys MC


 Choir Boys is not a show about America's dumbest criminals, but about America's smartest law enforcement officers. Each episode will feature a member in their 'day job' and in their 'colors.' Investigating a murder or busting a crack house in part of the episode, riding in a funeral procession for a fallen officer or a run to the Grand Canyon or Sturgis in the other half.
