Fallo! (2003)
Fallo! is a 2003 Italian film co-written and directed by Tinto Brass. The film is known in English as Do It! (English translation of Fallo!) and Private.[1] It consists of a series of 6 independent vignettes.
1) Alibi
2) Montaggio Alternato
3) 2 Cuori & 1 Capanna
4) Botte d'Allegria
5) Honni Soit Qui Mal Y Pense
6) Dimme Porca Che Me Piaze
